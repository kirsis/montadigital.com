Monta Digital is an IT consulting studio. It launched in Riga, in 2010, as a one-man development shop. It has since grown to 3 people with an additional base of operations in Bergen, Norway.

We are a small but dedicated and professional team of developers.

Located next to the old town of Riga, we are inspired daily by the many beautiful Art Nouveau buildings in our vicinity. This inspiration shows in our work.

This has been our head office since the founding of the company in 2010. Its convenient location means we can quickly travel to and from Riga's IT hub, for meetings, demos and on-site consultations.
